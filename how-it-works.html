How Pocket-Market Works

Discover how to buy from local sellers or start selling your products with our smart marketplace

I'm a Buyer I'm a Seller

For Buyers

Find great deals from local sellers and trusted vendors across Kenya

1

Browse or Search

Explore products by category or use our smart search to find exactly what you need. Filter by location, price range, and seller ratings.

2

Check Product Details

View high-quality photos, detailed descriptions, seller information, and customer reviews. Our platform verifies all listings for authenticity.

3

Purchase or Message

Buy directly through our secure checkout or message the seller for more details. Choose between delivery or local pickup options.

Buyer Benefits

  • Verified Sellers: All sellers undergo our verification process
  • Secure Payments: Multiple payment options including M-Pesa
  • Local Focus: Support Kenyan businesses and reduce shipping costs

For Sellers

Grow your business by reaching thousands of potential customers across Kenya

1

Create Your Seller Account

Sign up as a seller, complete your profile, and verify your identity. This builds trust with potential buyers.

2

List Your Products

Use our simple listing tool to add products with photos, descriptions, and pricing. Get platform recommendations for optimal visibility.

3

Manage Sales

Receive orders, communicate with buyers, and get paid securely. Track your sales performance in your dashboard.

Seller Benefits

Smart Recommendations

Our system analyzes your products and suggests the best platforms (Facebook, WhatsApp groups, etc.) to maximize your sales.

Low Fees

Keep more of your profits with our competitive commission rates compared to other marketplaces.

Built-in Marketing

Get featured in our newsletters, social media, and targeted promotions to the right buyers.

Seller Support

Access our seller education resources and dedicated support team to help grow your business.
