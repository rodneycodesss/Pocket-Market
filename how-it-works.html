How Pocket Market Works

Pocket Market is a platform that empowers Kenyan youth to sell their hand-crafted products online, easily and safely. Here’s how you can get started and succeed!

1

Sign Up & Create Your Account

Register with your email and set up your seller profile. It’s free and takes less than 2 minutes.

2

List Your Hand-Crafted Products

Add details, photos, and prices for your unique products—like jewelry, art, crafts, or fashion. Your listings will be visible to buyers across Kenya.

3

Connect & Sell

Buyers contact you directly through the platform. Arrange payment and delivery in a way that works for both of you. Get tips and support from our community!

Why Use Pocket Market?

  • ✔️ 100% free for youth sellers
  • ✔️ Safe, supportive community
  • ✔️ Tips and resources for growing your business
  • ✔️ Showcase your talent to buyers across Kenya
