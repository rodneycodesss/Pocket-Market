About Pocket Market

Empowering Kenyan youth and artisans by connecting them with buyers worldwide

Pocket Market is a platform dedicated to empowering Kenyan youth and artisans by connecting them with buyers looking for unique, handmade products and creative services. Our mission is to support local talent, foster entrepreneurship, and make it easy for anyone to discover and purchase authentic African creations.

Our Mission

To provide a trusted marketplace for handmade goods and services, helping young entrepreneurs grow their businesses and reach new customers.

Our Vision

To become the leading platform for discovering and supporting local talent across Africa, starting with Kenya.
